Our Story

We Are Elara Tech Labs

A team of young, relentless builders on a mission to make artificial intelligence the most powerful tool for human progress — starting with education.

Origin

Born from a Belief That AI Changes Everything

Elara Tech Labs was founded in 2025 with a singular conviction: AI is not just a tool — it is a fundamental shift in how intelligence operates at scale. We didn't want to wait on the sidelines. We wanted to build.

Starting with education — one of the most impactful and underserved sectors for technology — we launched Stradike: an AI-powered study operating system that doesn't just tutor students but truly knows them. It remembers. It adapts. It grows with each learner.

Today, Elara Tech Labs is expanding its reach across industries, delivering AI agents, automation systems, and custom intelligent software to organizations that understand the imperative of the AI era.

We are young. We move fast. And we build with precision.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To build AI infrastructure that gives individuals and organizations the intelligence capabilities previously reserved for the world's largest technology companies. We democratize elite AI — making it accessible, applicable, and transformative.

Our Vision

A world where every student learns with a personal AI that understands them. Where every business operates with intelligent agents handling complexity. Where human potential is amplified by AI, not replaced by it. That is the future we are building.
